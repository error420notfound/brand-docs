# Color & Accessibility

All color combinations used in product and marketing materials must meet **WCAG 2.1 AA** at minimum. AAA is recommended for body text.

## Contrast Ratios

| Combination | Ratio | AA | AAA |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ink on Paper | 16.8:1 | ✅ | ✅ |
| White on Brand Blue | 4.7:1 | ✅ | ❌ |
| Brand Blue on Paper | 4.1:1 | ✅ (large text) | ❌ |
| Gray 500 on Paper | 4.5:1 | ✅ | ❌ |
| Flame on White | 3.2:1 | ⚠️ Large text only | ❌ |

## WCAG Requirements

- **AA Normal text** (< 18pt): 4.5:1
- **AA Large text** (≥ 18pt or 14pt bold): 3:1
- **AAA Normal text**: 7:1
- **AAA Large text**: 4.5:1

## Never Use Color Alone

Color must never be the **only** visual indicator of state or meaning. Always pair color with:

- An icon (✓ ✗ ⚠)
- A label ("Error", "Success")
- Pattern or shape difference

This ensures the UI is accessible to users with color vision deficiencies.
