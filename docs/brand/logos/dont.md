# What Not to Do

Protecting the integrity of our logo means following these rules consistently.

<div className="do-dont">
  <div className="do-dont__col do-dont__col--do">
    <div className="do-dont__header">✓ Do</div>
    <div className="do-dont__body">
      <ul>
        <li>Use the approved logo files without modification</li>
        <li>Maintain minimum clear space</li>
        <li>Use on approved background colors</li>
        <li>Scale proportionally</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div className="do-dont__col do-dont__col--dont">
    <div className="do-dont__header">✗ Don't</div>
    <div className="do-dont__body">
      <ul>
        <li>Stretch or distort the logo</li>
        <li>Rotate or skew</li>
        <li>Apply drop shadows, outlines, or effects</li>
        <li>Recolor to unapproved colors</li>
        <li>Place on busy, low-contrast backgrounds</li>
        <li>Separate the mark from the wordmark (use the mark-only variant instead)</li>
        <li>Animate without brand team approval</li>
        <li>Recreate the logo from scratch</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

## Incorrect Background Usage

The logo must always be legible. Minimum contrast ratio between logo and background: **3:1**.

If in doubt, use the white or dark version of the logo and apply it to a solid color background from our approved palette.
